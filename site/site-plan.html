Overview

Purpose

The website will provide a place to keep track of my work as I progress in skill, functioning as a portfolio that I can show to others. It wil also be a collection of websites where I have found inspiration. This can act as a resource for others with an interest in sewing and quilting.

Audience

My audience will be potential clients or employers who can view my work, and also friends who are interested in sewing to view my resources and find inspiration.
